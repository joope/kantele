Kantele - How to play

  • Swipe across strings to pluck or strum
  • Hold a string to mute it
  • Drag the tuning pegs to adjust pitch
  • Keyboard: 1–5 pluck, Q W E R T mute

Kanteleliitto (est. 1977) unites kantele professionals, enthusiasts, instrument builders, composers, and researchers. Its largest event, the international kantele competition, draws hundreds of participants from around the world every two years.

In 2026, the association lost its state funding for the first time — threatening its operations, the competition, and the future of kantele building as craftsmen retire without resources to pass on their skills.
